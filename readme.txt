Custom AI Chatbot with your own data, personality & branding.

== Description ==

Easily connect your website pages, documents and other data resources to train a 24/7 custom AI, integrated with ChatGPT for your business.

= Train your AI bot with your data =
Easily train your agent by uploading documents, website pages, or other live data sources like Google Sheets. Allowing your agent to always be aware of the latest updates.

= AI Assistant that really represent your business =
Our chatbots are completely customizable so you can specify what they sound like and talk about. Training is easy as your bot can be trained on website info, documents, data sources, and any specific instructions you provide.

= Website embed & powerful integrations =
Our chatbot has you covered wherever you talk to customers. Integrate through Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, Slack, and a growing number of social media inboxes to make sure you have all your bases covered.

= Take it to a live agent when needed =
Our dashboard lets you monitor all active chats and swap in a real team member when needed. We also provide notifications and a quick chat summary to your team whenever a customer needs to speak to a real person. This way you can offer your customers the best of both worlds.

This plugin lets you easily embed your [ResolveAI Chatbot](https://resolveai.co?utm_source=web&utm_medium=wordpress&utm_campaign=plugin "ResolveAI - Custom AI Chatbot with your own data") on your Wordpress website.

== Installation ==

1. Download and unzip the plugin
1. Upload the plugin folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
1. Go to AI Chatbot to get started
